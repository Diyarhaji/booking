    Information about the housing unit


    The phrase a "Stone's throw" truly describes this secluded cabin in the woods. Located directly on Fightingtown Creek, which is known for its stocked trout fishing, you could literally throw out a line from the back porch and reel one in.


    Pulling into the driveway you will notice the small, quaint firepit area perfect for a warm, cozy night counting the stars and listening to the crickets' chirp. Walking into the entrance of this home is an experience all its own; going through the lit, custom designed walkway down to the stairs to the custom front door sets the mood for your fantasy like getaway! Entering the home, you will notice is a little unique feature also as you come in on the top level. This area offers seating for ten as well as a desk space.


    Making your way downstairs to the main living level of the cabin you will be awe struck. The open concept living area and kitchen make the home feel very large and spacious. The living area offers a plush sofa, 40" flat screen TV, and direct access to the back deck. The kitchen has a custom island with seating for 3 as well as soapstone countertops, kitchen aid appliances, and modern fridge and freezer drawers built directly into the island. Just off the kitchen area you will find a half bath with custom granite wall.


    As you continue through the home, you enter the master suite! This suite style bedroom offers a plush king-sized bed, 42" flat panel TV, seating area, and a full bathroom with tub/ shower combo. The master suite also has full deck access to both the private hot tub deck as well as access to the full back deck area.


    Finally, the back-deck area. This spot is sure to be the favorite! Creek view, gas grill and perfect seating for ten! Sit back, relax, and listen to the creek flow or walk down the 76 stairs to get Creekside and a closer look.
